Unfortunately, there's no recovery option — by design. This is zero-knowledge architecture. We never see or store your master password. You'll need to reset the extension and reconfigure your secrets. We strongly recommend writing down your secrets and storing them safely offline.

Traditional password managers store encrypted passwords in a vault. mindVault is different — we don't store passwords at all. We store recipes that combine with your secrets to generate passwords on demand. This means:

  • No password database to hack
  • Works with your existing Google Sheets workflow
  • You control the "formula" for each password
  • Passwords are never stored, even encrypted

  • bank$3 — Hash "bank" + Secret 3 as suffix
  • fb@2 — Hash "fb" + Secret 2 in the middle
  • gmail#1_v2 — Same recipe, version 2 (for rotation)
